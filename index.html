Biography

Xiaonan Luo received his Ph.D. degree in Computational Mathematics from Dalian University of Technology in 1991 and did postdoctoral research at Xidian University from 1992 to 1995. He joined Sun Yat-sen University as an Associate Professor in March 1995. He is currently a Professor of School of Electronics and Information Technology and also serves as Director of National Engineering Research Center of Digital Life, Director of State-Province Joint Laboratory of Digital Home Interactive Applications, and Director of Digital Home Standards Committee on Interactive Applications of China Electronics Standardization Association.

His research interests generally lie in Digital Home Technology, Mobile Computing, Computer Graphics & CAD and Image Processing. He has participated in more than 40 research programs funded by National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), Ministry of Education and provincial government. He has published more than 100 papers in international conferences and journals, and he is the holder of 200 patents, 20 software copyrights and 18 registered software products. He also guided the formulation of 2 state standards, 4 industry standards and 23 local standards.

Prof. Luo was an adjunct professor at Hefei University of Technology and Macau University of Science and Technology. He was also an adjunct PhD supervisor at Hong Kong Polytechnic University and visiting fellow at Winston Salem State University (USA). He served as an editorial board member of Journal of Information and Computational Science, Journal of Computational Information Systems and Journal of Mathematical Research with Applications. He was the co-chair of Sports Medicine and Rehabilitation Therapy-TBIS (2008).

Prof. Luo was granted the government special allowance by the State Council of China, and the National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars by the National Natural Science Foundation of China. He won the 1st Class Award in Science & Technology Progress by the Ministry of Education of China in 2003 and the 2nd Class Award in Science & Technology Progress by Ministry of Science and Technology of China, in 2004, China Patent Excellence Award by the State Intellectual Property Office of China, in 2010 and 2011.
